What Nextdriv Does

One platform from first plan to daily charge.

Nextdriv models your fleet the way it actually runs — routes, schedules, weather, batteries, and electricity tariffs — then keeps optimizing after deployment. No black boxes: our models are peer-reviewed and published.

Module 01

Plan — know before you buy

Test electrification scenarios on your own routes and walk into procurement with evidence instead of estimates.

  • Fleet mix, battery sizing, and vehicle requirements
  • Depot layout, charger count, and grid capacity
  • Total cost of ownership under real operating conditions
Module 02

Operate — charge at the lowest real cost

Schedule every vehicle’s charging around energy prices, grid limits, and tomorrow’s service.

  • Smart charging schedules that cut energy bills
  • Peak-demand reduction and energy-market participation
  • Degradation-aware strategies that extend battery life

Why Nextdriv?

Not a consultant. Not a charging platform. The decision layer for both.

01

Evidence before procurement

A consultant's study is static — it expires the day it's delivered. Nextdriv lets you test decisions on your own data before capital is committed, and keep testing as conditions change.

02

One platform, plan to operations

Most tools stop at planning or start after you've bought the assets. Nextdriv spans both, so the assumptions in your plan become the schedules in your depot.

03

Peer-reviewed models

Our optimization and battery-degradation models are published in IEEE Access, Energy, and Applied Energy — a methodology your procurement and engineering teams can defend.
