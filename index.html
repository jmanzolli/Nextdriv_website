Why Nextdriv?

Not a consultant. Not a charging platform. The decision layer for both.

01

Evidence before procurement

A consultant's study is static — it expires the day it's delivered. Nextdriv lets you test decisions on your own data before capital is committed, and keep testing as conditions change.

02

One platform, plan to operations

Most tools stop at planning or start after you've bought the assets. Nextdriv spans both, so the assumptions in your plan become the schedules in your depot.

03

Peer-reviewed models

Our optimization and battery-degradation models are published in IEEE Access, Energy, and Applied Energy — a methodology your procurement and engineering teams can defend.
