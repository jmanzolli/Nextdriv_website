Accessibility

We want nextdriv.ca to be usable by everyone. The site is built against WCAG 2.1 AA guidelines:

  • Semantic HTML with keyboard-navigable menus and visible focus states.
  • Content available without JavaScript.
  • Reduced-motion preferences respected for all animations.
  • Color contrast meeting AA on text elements.
  • Alternative text on informative images.

If you encounter a barrier on this site, email contact@nextdriv.ca and we will address it promptly and provide the information you need in an accessible format.
